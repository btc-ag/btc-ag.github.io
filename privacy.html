Privacy Policy

Since the protection of your privacy is a high priority for BTC AG, the use of this website is designed to be anonymous, as long as you do not voluntarily provide information about yourself. The data processing follows the following principles.

Collecting general information

When you access this website, general information is automatically tracked.
For technical reasons, when accessing this website your internet browser automatically transmits data to the web server. Each record consists of the date and time of access, the URL of the referring web site, the file retrieved, the amount of data sent, the browser type and version, the operating system and the user's IP address. An allocation of this data to a specific person is not possible. The stored data will only be evaluated internally and exclusively for statistical purposes.

GitHub transmits the following data to BTC AG:

  • Number of visitors to the btc-ag.github.io website and its child pages
  • Referrer of the visit
  • Date and time of the visit
  • Visited pages within our website btc-ag.github.io

The following data are additionally collected by GitHub:

  • IP address of the visitor
  • Technical information, such as operating system and browser of visitors
  • Language of the browser
  • Further visited pages within the GitHub offer

These data are not provided to us. They are collected and used exclusively by GitHub. You can read more about this in the GitHub Privacy Policy.

To protect the security of your data during transmission, GitHub uses state-of-the-art encryption techniques (such as SSL) over HTTPS.

Cookies

Like many other websites, GitHub also uses so-called "cookies" to collect, as noted before, technical information. Cookies are small text files that are transferred from a website server to your hard drive. Because of this GitHub automatically receives certain data e.g. IP address, browser, operating system from your computer and your connection to the internet.

Cookies cannot be used to launch programs or to transfer viruses to a computer.

The data we collect will not be passed on to third parties.

Certainly, you can also use our website without cookies. Internet browsers are regularly set to accept cookies. You can deactivate the use of cookies at any time via the settings of your browser. Please use the help features of your internet browser to find out how to change these settings. Please note that some features of our website may not work if you have disabled the use of cookies.

GitHub login and GitHub registration

The information you provide, while signing in or registering for a GitHUB-Account, will be used for the purposes specified by GitHub.

These can be read explicitly in the GitHub Data Protection Regulation.

Contacting BTC AG

If you contact us by email, the information you provide will be stored for the purpose of processing the request and for possible follow-up questions.

All personal data arising in this context will be collected, processed and used in accordance with the applicable laws and regulations for the protection of personal data for the following reasons:

  • processing your request
  • execution of contracts
  • ensuring our justified business interests with regard to counseling and supporting customers and to carve a demand-oriented service.

The use of personal data for the purpose of advertising / market research as well as for the design of our services only takes place if you have expressly given us your consent. You have a right to recall your given consent at any time. The deletion of the stored personal data takes place:

  • when the user of the website and / or customer revokes the consent,
  • if they are no longer necessary for the fulfillment of the purpose for which they were stored or
  • if the storage is inadmissible for other legal reasons.

Data for billing and accounting purposes are not affected by a request for cancellation. Please contact our data protection officer. The contact details can be found at the bottom.

A transfer of your personal data to third parties, neither for commercial nor for non-commercial purposes, does not take place, unless we are legally obliged to do so.

Google Analytics

This website uses Google Analytics, a web analytics service provided by Google Inc. ("Google"). Google Analytics uses so-called "cookies", text files that are stored on your computer to permit an analysis of your use of the website. Usually, the information generated by the cookie about your use of this website is transmitted to a server of Google in the US and is stored there. On behalf of GitHub, Google will use this information to evaluate your use of the website, to compile reports on website activity and to provide other services related to website activity and internet usage to the website operator. The IP address provided by your browser in the context of Google Analytics will not be merged with other data from Google. You can prevent the storage of cookies by a corresponding setting of your browser software. You can also prevent the collection and processing of data by Google by downloading the browser AddOn available under the following link: https://tools.google.com/dlpage/gaoptout

Your rights for information, rectification, detent, deletion and opposition

You have the right to receive information about your personal data stored by us at any time. Likewise, you have the right to correct, block or delete your personal data, with the exception for the required data storage for business transactions.

In order to be able to ensure a data block at any time, the data must be kept in a lock file for control purposes. You can also request the deletion of the data, if there is no legal obligation for archiving. If there is an obligation, we can block your data on your demand.

You may suspend or revoke your consent by notifying us with effect for the future.

Changes of our Privacy Policy

We reserve the right to change this privacy policy from time to time to ensure that it is always complies with legal requirements or to implement changes to our services in the privacy policy, e.g. introducing new services. The new privacy policy is valid for your next visit.
